www.princesssubmarines.ca

princesssubmarines.ca was my first experience building a website when I was 17. Being a former employee at the subshop, I new a lot about this business and therefore was able to create a website with an appropriate user interface and user experience flow. I used wordpress to create this design and learned a lot about how Search Engine Opitimization can influence the visibility of a site. This website is still up and running and is something I continue to monitor.

Ministry of Health

Out of Country Prior Approval Online Application System

This past summer of 2023 I held a position at the Ontario Minitstry of Health working to standardize webinterface designs. Working closely with a small team of developers we worked to redesign both the UI and UX of government websites (specifically the Out of Country Prior Approval Application System). I worked on figma to create modernized and accessible designs that will be implimented in all government sites moving forward.
